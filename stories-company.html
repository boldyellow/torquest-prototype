The Pinova Story

Pinova Holdings is an industry leader, a global producer and marketer of specialty chemicals derived from natural, renewable materials such as pine trees and citrus peels. We acquired Pinova, Inc. from Ashland, Inc. in 2010.

How We Helped

We have provided counsel and invested considerable capital to support management's strategies to add people, expand manufacturing capacity and make acquisitions to develop and grow the business. The first acquisition was Renessenz, a leading producer of fragrances and flavors using natural ingredients, which significantly broadened Pinova’s expertise and offerings.

Pinova is thriving today, with solid, consistent growth in revenue, earnings and market share.
