Pat Grozier On Trust
“These people are straight shooters. From day one, they did exactly what they said they’d do.”

We first heard about Thinking Capital through our network of Canadian executives, one of whom told us we should look into them. The business, a financial technology company specializing in financing for small to medium-sized enterprises, was intriguing and we were extremely impressed with founder/CEO Jeff Mittleman and his team. But for a number of reasons, we did not see a fit for TorQuest.

But we stayed in touch. A year later the opportunity came for TorQuest to take a majority position and we closed the transaction. In addition to capital, we have since supported Thinking Capital's growth through a go-to-market strategy based on channel partnerships with large retail institutions. On the strength of TorQuest•s relationships, Thinking Capital signed a referral arrangement with the CDIC for rapid small business financing and has a number of similar deals pending.

With TorQuest's help, Thinking Capital has transformed itself from a small, grass-roots financing provider to partnering with major institutions to their mutual benefit—and becoming the largest and strongest company of its kind in Canada.
