Thinking Capital

Financial Technology

Overview

Founded in 2006, Thinking Capital is a leading financial technology company that provides financing to small and medium-sized enterprises ("SME") across Canada. Thinking Capital is the country's largest provider of merchant cash advances, a financing structure that appeals to SMEs with a high volume of daily electronic transactions, such as retailers and restaurants, because the structure has the flexibility to ebb and flow with the performance of the merchant's business.
